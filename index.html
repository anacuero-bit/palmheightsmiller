The Case for Action

The cost of staying still
is no longer zero.

Geo-political instability, tightening tax regimes, and closing residency windows are compressing the timeline for decisive action.

01

Single-Jurisdiction Exposure

73% of HNWIs hold assets in one country. A single regulatory shift — capital controls, wealth taxes, CBI closures — can erode decades of wealth in months. Spain's golden visa closed April 2025. Portugal ended its real estate route. Which program closes next?

02

Passport Depreciation

Global mobility is diverging. While some passports gain access, others lose it. The gap between the most and least mobile nationalities has never been wider. Residency options available today carry no guarantee of existing tomorrow.

03

Escalating Tax Transparency

CRS, FATCA, BEPS 2.0, and global minimum tax frameworks are eliminating opacity. Strategic residency structuring — not secrecy — is the only compliant path to tax efficiency. The window to restructure is narrow and closing.
